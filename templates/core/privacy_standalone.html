Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account
  • List items for donation
  • Request clothing items
  • Communicate with other users
  • Subscribe to our newsletter

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Facilitate clothing exchanges between users
  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Send you newsletters and promotional communications

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information:

  • With other users to facilitate exchanges (limited contact information)
  • With service providers who assist our operations
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of promotional communications
  • Request a copy of your data

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
